A next-of-kin box is a single, findable place that holds what the people you
trust will need if you die or become unable to speak for yourself. This guide
walks through what to include, section by section, with printable checklists.

Work through the sections in any order. Nothing you enter is stored or sent
anywhere — this site has no accounts and no backend.
